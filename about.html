About Journey Trades

An independent sourcing partner, not another exporter.

We're a small, Pakistan-based team that works exclusively for international buyers — finding, verifying, and coordinating textile manufacturers so you don't have to do it from a distance, alone.

International buyers don't need another exporter adding a margin on top of the factory price. They need an independent team in Pakistan who works for the buyer, not the factory.

How We Work

We sit on your side of the table.

Journey Trades is based in Sargodha, Punjab — inside the region we source from, not outside it. That's the entire point: we can pick up the phone, visit a factory, or sit across the table from a supplier when you can't.

Independence

We don't own a factory, represent one exclusively, or take a margin on the goods you buy. Our work is the sourcing and verification service itself — agreed with you before we start.

Verification Over Claims

A factory's word is a starting point, not a conclusion. We check documented capacity, speak with management directly, and visit in person when a project calls for it.

Plain Communication

No jargon, no overselling. If something in your brief isn't realistic — an MOQ, a price point, a timeline — we'll tell you early, not after you've committed.

Realistic Scope

We're a focused, early-stage operation working one textile niche carefully, rather than claiming to cover every category or country. What we take on, we follow through on.

Setting Expectations

What we are — and what we're not

Being clear about scope is part of being trustworthy. Here's what Journey Trades does and doesn't do.

We are

→ An outsourced sourcing team working on your behalf
→ Specialists in Pakistani textile & home-textile manufacturing
→ Based on the ground, available for factory visits
→ Paid for the sourcing and verification service itself

We are not

→ A factory, manufacturer, or trading company reselling goods
→ A freight forwarder or customs clearance agent
→ Exclusively tied to or paid by any one factory
→ Active outside textiles — we don't currently cover other industries
