FAQ

Questions buyers ask us before we start.

If your question isn't answered here, send it to us directly — WhatsApp and email both reach us within one business day.

We act as your outsourced sourcing team inside Pakistan. We find, evaluate, and verify textile manufacturers on your behalf, coordinate sampling and communication, and — where useful — visit factories in person. We do not manufacture goods ourselves and we are not a trading company buying and reselling textiles.
We review a factory's documented capacity, production lines, and relevant certifications, speak directly with factory management, and where the project justifies it, visit the site in person. We look at what a factory can actually produce at the quality and volume you need — not just what they claim in an email.
Yes. Factory visits and written and photo reporting are one of our core services. We scope visit frequency and depth to the project — a single verification visit for a smaller order, ongoing visits for in-progress production.
Yes. We support custom sourcing projects, including matching your specification or sample to factories with the right capability, and coordinating development samples before production.
It depends on the product and the factory. Many established Pakistani textile manufacturers set MOQs based on production efficiency, not arbitrary preference. We're upfront with you about realistic MOQ ranges for your product before you commit to a sourcing project, and we look for factories whose MOQ structure fits your order size where possible.
We don't publish a fixed price list, because every sourcing project is different. Pricing depends on the complexity of what you're sourcing, how many suppliers need to be evaluated, whether factory visits are required, how deep the verification needs to go, and what reporting you need along the way. We discuss your brief first and quote based on the actual scope.
No. We don't take a margin on the products you purchase from a factory. Our work is the sourcing and verification service itself, scoped and agreed with you before we start.
Yes — this is one of the most common reasons buyers come to us. If you're looking to diversify away from a single country or factory, or you've had a bad experience with an existing supplier, we build a shortlist of vetted alternatives in Pakistan that match your current specification.
We review what you've sent, ask any clarifying questions by WhatsApp or email, and come back with a proposed scope and approach — including roughly how many suppliers we'd evaluate and what verification would look like — before any commercial commitment is made.
No. Our work covers sourcing, supplier verification, sampling, and factory coordination — not logistics. We flag this clearly during scoping so you know to arrange freight and customs separately, and we're happy to share what we know from experience if it's useful.
Right now we work exclusively within textiles — cotton home textiles (towels, bed linen), woven and knit fabric, and garments. We don't currently source outside textiles.
