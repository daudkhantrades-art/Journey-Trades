Talk With Our Team
Services

Six services. One sourcing team in Pakistan.

Each service can stand alone or combine into a full sourcing engagement — scoped to your project, not sold as a fixed package.

Describe Your Project

What each service covers

01

Supplier Discovery

You tell us what you need — product, specification, target quality and price point, rough volume. We identify Pakistani manufacturers genuinely capable of producing it and bring you a short, qualified list.

  • Matching against your specification, not a generic factory directory
  • Initial screening for relevant production capability
  • A shortlist with context on each candidate
02

Factory Verification

Before you commit time or money to a factory, we check what it claims against what it can actually do.

  • Review of documented production capacity and relevant certifications
  • Direct conversation with factory management
  • In-person visit where the project justifies it
03

Sampling Coordination

We brief the factory on your requirement, track sample development, and check what comes off the line before it's shipped to you.

  • Spec sheet translated into production-ready instructions
  • Sample progress tracked against your timeline
  • Pre-shipment check against your original requirement
04

Supplier Communication Support

We sit between you and the factory floor — clarifying specifications, chasing updates, and translating where needed, so nothing gets lost between time zones.

  • A single point of contact working in your timezone
  • Specification questions clarified before they become mistakes
  • Regular status updates, not radio silence
05

Factory Visits & Reporting

When a project justifies it, we visit the factory in person and report back honestly on what we saw — production status, quality observations, and anything that looks like a risk.

  • Photo and written reporting after each visit
  • Frequency scoped to your order — a single check, or ongoing monitoring
  • Direct, unfiltered observations — not a sales summary
06

Custom Sourcing Projects

Have a specific product, sample, or spec that doesn't fit a standard category? We scope custom projects individually and find factories matched to that exact brief.

  • One-off projects, not just recurring orders
  • Development support for new product specifications
  • Scoped and quoted on its own terms

How pricing works

We don't publish a fixed price list, because every sourcing project is different. What it costs depends on:

— the complexity of what you're sourcing
— how many suppliers need to be evaluated
— whether factory visits are required
— how deep the verification needs to go
— what reporting you need along the way

We review your brief first and quote based on the actual scope — never the other way around.
