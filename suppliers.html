Why Work With Us

What partnering with us actually changes

We're not a directory and we don't sell leads. We work with a small number of verified factories and bring them buyers whose briefs we've already screened.

No Upfront Fee

There's no cost to apply or to be considered as a verified partner. We don't charge factories to be evaluated.

Pre-Screened Enquiries

Buyer briefs are reviewed before they reach you, so you're not spending time on enquiries that were never a real fit.

No Reseller Markup Confusion

We don't take a margin on your goods. Commercial terms are agreed directly between you and the buyer.

A Single Point of Contact

We handle the buyer-side communication and translation where needed, so production time isn't spent managing email threads.

Relationship, Not One-Off Leads

We aim to build ongoing relationships with a small number of factories we trust — not a one-time introduction.

Verified Status

Going through our verification process is itself a credibility signal you can use with other buyers, not just the ones we introduce.

What We Look For

Verification criteria

We're selective because our credibility with buyers depends on it. Here's what we check before introducing a factory.

01

Genuine production capacity

You operate the production lines yourself — we work with manufacturers, not trading or middleman setups presenting another factory's capacity as their own.

02

Documentation on request

Business registration, production capability details, and any relevant certifications you hold, available for review when asked.

03

Open to a verification visit or call

A willingness to host a factory visit or take a verification call — this is how we confirm capability before introducing you to a buyer.

04

Export-ready, or working toward it

Currently exporting, or able to meet international buyer requirements with reasonable preparation.

05

Responsive communication

Able to communicate clearly in English, or through a translator, with reasonably prompt turnaround on buyer questions.
