2. Description of Services

Journey Trades provides outsourced sourcing and supplier-development services for international textile buyers, including but not limited to:

  • Supplier discovery, identification, and qualification within Pakistan's textile and home-textile manufacturing base
  • Factory verification, including review of documented capacity and, where agreed, in-person factory visits
  • Sampling coordination and pre-shipment sample review
  • Supplier communication support between the client and factory
